Cheeseburger

This is one freaking massive cheeseburger bro.

This is a recipe for making a gigantic, juicy, and above all else delicious, double bacon cheeseburger.

Ingredients

  • 85% Ground Beef
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Seasonings
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • Bacon
  • Cheese
  • Mayo
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Portion out hamburger meat into 1/3 pound balls
  2. Press balls into patties
  3. Season each patty with your seasonings
  4. Cook bacon on pan or oven
  5. Cook on preheated pan or grill to your desired doneness
  6. Before removing, place a slice of cheese on each of your patties and let melt
  7. Remove burgers from cooktop and let rest for a few minutes
  8. In the meantime, prepare onions and pickles and top buns with mayo and ketchup
  9. Place cooked bacon on top of burgers, and then onto prepared buns
  10. Enjoy!
